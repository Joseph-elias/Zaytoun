Data Consent Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

When you use Zaytoun, you consent to the processing of data required to provide platform services.

  • We process account data (name, phone, role) to authenticate and manage access.
  • We process operational data (bookings, market, olive records) to power features you use.
  • Location data is optional and only used for matching and map-based workflows when provided.
  • Uploaded images are processed to enable product and diagnostic features.
  • You can request account deletion, which removes your user account and related records according to system rules.
